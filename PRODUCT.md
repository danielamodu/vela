# Product

## Register

product

## Users
AI researchers, Web3 data brokers, and casual data enthusiasts looking to publish, discover, and subscribe to AI training datasets.

## Product Purpose
A verifiable AI dataset marketplace on the Sui blockchain with Walrus decentralized storage. It allows users to confidently trade, buy, and explore datasets.

## Brand Personality
Simple, straightforward, and highly functional.

## Anti-references
AI slop, vibe-coded websites, generic AI dashboards.

## Design Principles
1. Clarity over cleverness
2. Focus on the data
3. Reliable and verifiable

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard WCAG web best practices.
